my skills

At fifty-one, I switched my career into tech, left the boatbuilding industry to find a new path in life.

My name is Patrick Svensson and I chose frontend development and UX/UI design, where I can still use my creativity and analytic skills to seek clever solutions to problems, but in a digital context. It is something I have enjoyed doing as a boatbuilder as well as a bar manager.

I am an empathetic and aesthetic person, and therefore, found a great passion for UX/UI design. My ambition is to work with UCD and Design Thinking, conduct UX research, run user tests, create mockups and prototypes. My work experiences have given me a keen eye for details, and I am determined to keep learning about layout, branding, typography, colour palettes, interface components and more.

The successful management- and team roles in my career (read more on my LinkedIn page) show that I take responsibility, am capable of working independently, yet collaborate easily, with an open mind that constantly wants to learn more and find new ways to improve my work. My communication skills are well suited for holding presentations and workshops.

I live in a traditional sailing boat, moored up on the east coast of Sweden not far from Stockholm. Here, I enjoy the Baltic Sea, and cooking delicious vegetarian food together with my partner Helena and the ship’s cat Mina.
