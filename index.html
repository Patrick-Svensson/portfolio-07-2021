...my projects

As fate would have it, at fifty-one, I switched my career into tech. To keep the osteoarthritis in my hands at bay, I had to leave the boatbuilding industry behind and find a new path in life.

I chose front-end development and UX/UI design, where I can still use my creativity and analytic skills to seek clever solutions to problems, but in a digital context. It is something I have enjoyed doing as a boatbuilder as well as a bar manager.

Since I’m an aesthetic and service-minded person with strong visual skills, I have taken a special interest in UX/UI design with insights into user tests, A/B tests, and design thinking. I collaborate easily, always with an open mind that constantly wants to learn more and find new ways to improve my work.

Many years of work experience have taught me to interpret and understand the needs of the customer, take responsibility for my work, and given me an outstandingly high work ethic.

I live in a traditional sailing boat, moored up on the east coast of Sweden not far from Stockholm, where I enjoy sailing and cooking delicious vegetarian food together with my partner Helena and the ship’s cat Mina.
